How Custom Notes Work
1. Place your PDF file in the project root folder (d:\KLbiotech\)
2. Go to Add Note and fill in the filename exactly as it appears
3. Your note will appear in the Notes browser and Search immediately
4. Custom notes are saved in your browser — export to share with others
